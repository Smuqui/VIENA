dataFile="<your datafile>",
logFile="<your logfile>",
baudRate=9600,
loglevel=<logging level int>)
Default values:
    comPort="/dev/ttyUSB0"
    dataFile="./data/out.csv"
    logFile="output.log"
    baudRate=9600
    logFevel=20

    WARNING  |  30
    INFO     |  20
    DEBUG    |  10
    NOTSET   |  0
    -----------------------
